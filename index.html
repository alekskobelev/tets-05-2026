How It Works

Three simple steps to transform your physical parts into digital models

1

Scan the Part

We carefully scan your mechanical component using high-end 3D scanning equipment. The process captures millions of data points to ensure complete accuracy.

2

Analyze the Data

Our experts analyze and process the raw scan data using professional analysis software. We refine the data, remove noise, and prepare it for CAD conversion.

3

Create CAD Model

We generate a precise, editable CAD model from the analyzed data. The final model is ready for manufacturing, replication, or further modifications.
